Hour of Code

Details of the project:

  • Date & Time: November 8th, 15:00
  • Place: "WorkLab" Coworking Center
  • Participants: 9 kids from Nasimi district, Azadliq

Work distribution:

  • Amil Salahov: Team Leader, organized participants, booked venue, helped with the report
  • Rufat Ahadov: Main instructor, provided presentation, and helped with the report
  • Yusif Mammadli: Assistant teacher, helped participants and assisted Rufat
  • Nurjan Aliguluyeva: Photographer, videographer, assistant teacher, helped with the report

Preparation steps:

  1. Created agenda for the event.
  2. Developed a QR code for the maze exercise on code.org.
  3. Designed a clear teaching procedure for the exercise.
  4. Prepared a presentation about ADA University.
  5. Printed participation certificates for attendees.
  6. Set up all systems at the venue before the event.

More details:

Initially, our team expected to have over 20 participants in this event. however, due to some unanticipated
circumstances, this did not happen, and only 9 kids were able to participate. The event went well, we started
by giving brief introduction into how do algorithms work, then went through each level of code.org's "Maze"
course, and wrapped up by giving innoductory presentation about ADA University and distributing the certificates.
We have received a lot of positive feedback from the event! You can see the highlights in the Instagram post on the right.
